# Competitive Intelligence

Research and analyze competitor strategies, advertising, messaging, and market positioning. Gather data from public sources, identify patterns in successful campaigns, and produce actionable competitive insights.

## Overview

Comprehensive competitor analysis toolkit that transforms raw competitive data into strategic insights. Covers:
- Competitor messaging and positioning
- Advertising strategies and creative patterns
- Pricing and packaging models
- Social media and content strategies
- Market positioning and differentiation

## Workflow

1. **Define scope** — Confirm target competitors, industry, channels, and timeframe
2. **Gather data** — Collect ads, landing pages, pricing, messaging from public sources
3. **Analyze patterns** — Apply the framework to each competitor
4. **Synthesize insights** — Compare across competitors, surface trends and gaps
5. **Report** — Deliver findings with clear recommendations

## Data Sources

### Primary Routes
- **WebSearch + WebFetch** — Default approach, always available
  - Competitor websites, landing pages, pricing pages
  - Review sites (G2, Capterra, TrustRadius)
  - Public announcements, press releases, blog posts
  - Job postings (reveal strategic priorities)

### Ad Research
- Third-party ad reports and screenshots (more reliable than automation)
- Landing pages linked from ads
- Public social media profiles
- Ad intelligence summaries from blogs and case studies

## Analysis Framework

Evaluate each competitor across:

### Messaging
- Problems highlighted
- Value propositions
- Positioning statement
- Proof points

### Creative Patterns
- Visual style and brand consistency
- Ad formats (static, carousel, video)
- CTAs and urgency language
- Landing page structure

### Copy Formulas
- Headline structures
- Tone (formal/casual, technical/accessible)
- Length and hooks

### Pricing & Packaging
- Business model (freemium, trial, demo-gated, usage-based)
- Tier structure and feature differentiation
- Price anchoring strategies

## Output Options

- **Markdown Report** (default) — Executive summary, competitor profiles, comparisons
- **XLSX Spreadsheet** — Structured data with multiple sheets for analysis
- **PPTX Presentation** — Slide deck for stakeholder sharing

## Typical Deliverables

- Executive summary with 3-5 key insights
- Per-competitor deep dives
- Side-by-side comparison matrix
- Competitive gaps and opportunities
- Actionable recommendations

## Legal & Ethics

- Use only **publicly accessible** information
- Do not copy competitor creative or copy verbatim
- Do not attempt to access gated/private content
- Respect intellectual property rights
- Clearly label findings as research, not verified claims

## When to Use

- Launching a new product or feature
- Repositioning your brand
- Pricing strategy review
- Marketing campaign planning
- Sales competitive battlecards
- Market research and strategy
